Questions

Is this really free?

Yes. No signup, no email capture, no upsell. I built this on a weekend. If it helps you avoid a $10k mistake, tell a friend.

How accurate is the grade?

It's grounded in real NHTSA recall data, owner complaint patterns, and current market pricing — not vibes. That said, it's not a substitute for an in-person inspection. Always get a pre-purchase inspection (PPI) from a mechanic before you buy.

What if I don't have the VIN?

VIN is optional. Without it I work off the year/make/model/trim from the listing text. With it I can pull recall-specific data.

Do you save my data?

No. The listing text is sent for analysis and then it's gone. I'm not building a database.

Why are you doing this?

Marketing for my detailing business and honestly — I think it's fun. Most of my customers buy used. If this stops one of you from buying a salvage-title Altima, worth it.
